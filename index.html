Hello! I am Valerie Martinez, a UX/ UI Designer new to the field, but ready to work hard and create awesome combinations of user experiences and interfaces for projects I am a part of. I enjoy the ability of design to engage and inspire people and I like solving user problems by asking lots of relevant questions and always thinking about the users engagement with a product. With my background in the fine arts, I am excited to bring what I’ve learned to do on paper to what I’ve learned to do on the screens.

My UX Skills

User Research

My approach to user research includes asking users the necessary questions for each phase of research but also extra questions like their impressions on certain parts of an interface or navigation just to keep gauging the impression of the product throughout its stages.

User Empathy

Considering the users feelings for a product is very important to understand so I like to gather everything I can when listening to feedback during research. When looking at feedback I like to look closely at identified pain points and think of ways to relieve them through better visual design and information architecture.

Qualitative Research

Gaining qualitative data is important to gather as well as quantitative data because it gives insight into why a product isn’t working as well as it could be. It’s important for the design process that during the research phase, this information is gathered so that important design choices can be reflected.

My Work

Underdog Animal Rescue - Nonprofit Website Redesign

This non-profit website redesign was a fun project for my team and I because the website was for a dog rescue. The redesign sought to help make calls to actions more accessible to help adoption and donation rates, and create cleaner UI and easier navigation.

U.S. Department of Education - Government Website Redesign

This government website redesign was targeted by my team and I because we noticed this website could use an updated UI, easier navigation, and information architecture. We sought to help some pain points and accessibility issues with the text and some responsive issues.

Pet Pro - Mobile App Design

This was a project born from an identified need to keep track of veterinary records for our pets. The need was identified by pet owners we interviewed who needed a better way to keep the important medical records and appointments up to date for their pets.
